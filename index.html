About Me

A Different Kind of Agent

I am Peter Luo — a Greater Toronto Area real estate agent with RE/MAX Realtron Realty Inc. I hold the SRS (Seller Representative Specialist), MCNE (Master Certified Negotiation Expert), and FRI (Fellow of the Real Estate Institute) designations, and I serve both English and Chinese-speaking clients across the GTA.

"I do not show up as a high-pressure salesperson. I show up as your calm, data-driven market consultant — with a proven system behind me."

My approach is grounded in market intelligence, careful preparation, and skilled negotiation. I believe that when sellers are well-informed and properly supported, better outcomes follow naturally — higher prices, smoother transactions, and far less stress.

The Pinnacle Listing System

Designed to Deliver
Three Things

Most sellers want the same three things: the best possible price, the least possible stress, and a sale that happens in a reasonable amount of time. The Pinnacle Listing System was built specifically around these three goals — not as a slogan, but as a structured process.

  • 01
    Comprehensive Market Analysis

    I begin with a rigorous, data-backed CMA using current TRREB statistics. You receive an honest, transparent picture of your property's true market value — not a number designed to win your listing.

  • 02
    Strategic Property Positioning

    Together we identify your home's unique strengths and address any concerns proactively. Presentation, staging, photography, and pricing strategy are all calibrated to maximise buyer competition.

  • 03
    Targeted Multi-Channel Marketing

    Your property is promoted across MLS, major real estate portals, digital and social media, and my exclusive Chinese-language network — reaching every motivated buyer in the GTA.

  • 04
    Expert Offer Management & Negotiation

    Every offer is evaluated with professional discipline. I guide you through each scenario — single offer, multiple offers, conditional offers — to extract the strongest possible outcome.
