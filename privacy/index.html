Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how peterluo.homes ("we", "us") and its related publishing tools handle information. It applies to this website and to the automated tools we operate to publish our own marketing content to third-party platforms.

Who we are

peterluo.homes is the website of Peter Luo, a licensed real estate professional serving Richmond Hill and York Region. You can reach us at peter@peterluo.homes.

Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account to view this site. We may collect limited, non-identifying analytics (such as pages viewed and general location) to understand how the site is used. If you contact us directly, we receive the details you choose to send, such as your name, email, and message.

Our social publishing automation

We operate a private automation that publishes our own marketing content — property photos, listing updates, and market commentary — to social and content platforms that we own or administer (for example Pinterest, and our own channels). This automation uses each platform's official API under credentials tied to our own accounts. It publishes only content we create, only to destinations we control, and does not access, collect, store, or post any other person's private data.

Third-party platforms

When we publish to third-party platforms, your interaction with that content is governed by that platform's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any platform where you engage with our posts.

How we use information

We use the limited information described above only to operate and improve the site, respond to inquiries, and provide real-estate services you request. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep contact information only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or provide requested services, and analytics data for a limited period.

Your choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, to request a correction, or to request deletion, by emailing peter@peterluo.homes.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.
